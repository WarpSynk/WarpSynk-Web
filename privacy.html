Privacy Policy

At WarpSynk, we believe in data sovereignty and your right to control your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in our decentralized ecosystem.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

WarpSynk Protocol ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized data synchronization protocol, website, and services.

As a Web3 project built on principles of data sovereignty, we strive to minimize data collection while providing a secure and efficient synchronization layer for Web2 and Web3 applications.

Information We Collect

In keeping with our data sovereignty principles, we collect minimal information:

  • Wallet Address: When you interact with our protocol, we collect your wallet address for transaction purposes.
  • On-Chain Data: We analyze public blockchain data related to protocol interactions.
  • Technical Information: We may collect browser type, device type, and IP address (anonymized) for security and analytics purposes.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us, we collect your email address and correspondence content.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our synchronization services
  • To monitor and analyze protocol performance and usage patterns
  • To prevent fraudulent activities and enhance security
  • To communicate with you about protocol updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To enforce our terms of service and other policies

Data Sovereignty Principles

As a data sovereignty-focused project, we adhere to these core principles:

  • User Control: You maintain control over your data at all times
  • Transparency: We clearly explain what data we collect and why
  • Minimization: We collect only what is necessary for protocol operation
  • Decentralization: Where possible, we avoid central points of data control
  • Security: We implement robust security measures to protect your information

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in protocol operation (under strict data protection terms)
  • When required by law or to respond to legal process
  • To protect the rights and property of WarpSynk, our users, or the public
  • In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition

All shared data is minimized and anonymized where possible.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Access controls limiting who can access user information
  • Smart contract audits and bug bounty programs

However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

International Data Transfers

As a decentralized protocol with global operations, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your country's laws.

When we transfer data across borders, we implement safeguards such as standard contractual clauses to ensure your data remains protected.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity only on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.

Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: The right to know what personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: The right to have inaccurate information corrected
  • Deletion: The right to have your personal information deleted
  • Portability: The right to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: The right to object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
